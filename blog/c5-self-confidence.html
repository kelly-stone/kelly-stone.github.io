blog 5 - Self-confidence

1. What is the relationship between mindfulness and self awareness?

Mindfulness is an activity that results in awareness. To be mindfulness is to discovering something that already yours and then developing and refining it through systematic cultivation and practice. Its like a practice strategy. Mindfulness and awareness always complement each other.

2. Try at least one of the self awareness practices from the chapter (Body Scan, Journaling). What was your experience of it? How did you feel?

The body scan is a component of mindfulness meditation. I have tried to simply lie down and do nothing, lying on my ned, focusing my attention at the top of my head and then move down the body. This trains attention, trains my mind to be able to move from detail attention to a wider awareness from one moment to the next.

3. What did you take away from the "My Emotions are Not Me" section?

Emotions are simply what we feel, not who we are, if our emotions are who we are, then there is very little we can do about it. “Possessing this insight, one creates the possibility of change within oneself.” Excerpt From: Tan, Chade-Meng. “Search Inside Yourself: The Unexpected Path to Achieving Success, Happiness (and World Peace).” iBooks.
